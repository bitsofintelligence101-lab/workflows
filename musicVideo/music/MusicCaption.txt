Upbeat 2020s art-pop with massive crossover energy and an instantly-recognizable hook. Intimate female vocal in the verses — close and present in the mix, clearly articulated and upfront — opening quickly into a soaring, anthemic chorus with bright, clear layered harmonies. Instrumentation blends shimmering layered synth pads, glitchy digital textures, a pulsing four-on-the-floor electronic beat, and warm organic piano touches grounding the digital textures in something human. Maximalist, polished modern production with controlled reverb that keeps vocals forward and intelligible, hyper-processed vocal stutters, and subtle pitch-bent vocal chops as accents. Short, punchy intro leading quickly into a euphoric, wall-of-sound chorus, brief stripped-back bridge, then final chorus hits even bigger. Dreamy, emotionally vulnerable mood — beauty blurring into unreality, like memory and simulation overlapping. Mid-to-uptempo hypnotic looping groove with a danceable but melancholic undertow, vocals always clear and present above the mix.
